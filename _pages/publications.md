My publications span multimodal verification, scientific and environmental NLP, crisis informatics, and domain-adapted language models. I am especially interested in work that connects model capability with grounded evidence, interpretability, and real-world decision support.

## Selected Themes

- **Video and multimodal verification:** grounding inconsistencies between generated or retrieved text and visual evidence.
- **Domain-specific LLMs:** adapting and evaluating language models for chemistry, scientific corpora, and environmental review.
- **Crisis and misinformation modeling:** using NLP and multimodal features to understand crisis-related social media and fake-news stance.
